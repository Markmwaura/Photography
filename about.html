About

At his core, Ian is an artist. Starting out as a painter first, Ian fell in love with the creative process. He then went on to study graphic design in college and founded his own graphic design company, Pixelgrazer, in 2001. Ian really only began taking pictures to bring texture into his design work. But before he knew it, he realized that photography was his true passion. Ian has taken portraits of many familiar such as Taylor Swift, Kelly Clarkson and Tim Tebow. He also likes taking photos of nature and landscape.
